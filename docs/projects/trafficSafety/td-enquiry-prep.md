# TD Enquiry — Briefing Note (Internal)

**Audience:** Talia + Kelly + Zehua + Simon. NOT for forwarding to TD.
**Companion:** `td-desk-research.md` (what TD has already published).

We get effectively ONE high-quality response from TD per Code on Access request. After triage against desk research, our 14-question draft compresses to **5 sharp questions + 1 data ask**. Everything else is either already public or guaranteed to be deflected.

## The 5 questions we will actually send

> Each is phrased so a deflection is itself informative — i.e. the absence of a document is a finding we can cite in the project report.

**Q1 — Origin & rationale of the Junction Blacksite list**
Please provide any internal circular, working paper, or memo (redacted as needed) that records the *original policy intention* for establishing the Junction Blacksite list (referenced in LegCo LCQ14, 2 July 2008). If no such document exists, please confirm that the criteria were set as an operational decision without a founding policy paper.

**Q2 — Addition of Category C (≥8 pedestrian-injury accidents in 3 years)**
Category C first appears in the quarterly list between Q4-2018 and Q3-2023. Please provide (a) the exact quarter Category C took effect, (b) the decision document or minute authorising the addition, and (c) any public announcement issued at the time.

**Q3 — Standard remediation workflow per blackspot**
For a junction newly classified under Category A, please describe the *standard workflow* (steps, responsible units, target timelines) from classification to completion of remediation works. We are aware of the aggregate figures in LCQ13 (30 July 2025: ~70 sites 2020–24, ~50 completed) and are asking for the **process**, not the totals.

**Q4 — Site-specific record for two named junctions, 2015–2024**
For (a) Chatham Road South × Austin Road × Cheong Wan Road and (b) Kweilin Street × Tai Nan Street, please provide the years each junction was on the blacksite list and the **dates and nature of any improvement works** completed at each location during 2015–2024.

**Q5 — Pre-2014 vs post-2014 reporting methodology**
HKPF and C&SD statistical releases divide accident data at 2013/2014. Please confirm whether TD's accident recording methodology or system changed in or around 2013–14, and provide any methodology note explaining the discontinuity.

**Data ask — Junction-approach AADT (separate Code request)**
Annual Average Daily Traffic, by approach, for the 21 junctions listed in Annex A (we provide the list), for available years 2014–2024. If turning-movement counts are not routinely recorded, please confirm what is held.
