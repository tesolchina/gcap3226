# Transport Department — Desk Research Before Enquiry

**Internal reference for teaching team + Fall 2026 road-safety students.**
Compiled June 2026. All URLs verified at time of writing.

Purpose: know what TD has ALREADY published so we don't waste questions in our Code on Access enquiry asking for things that are one click away.

---

## 1. Is there a standalone "Junction Blacksite policy" document?

**No.** TD lists the blacksite data quarterly but has never published a separate policy statement / circular / gazette notice explaining the *origin* or *rationale* of the list. The definition is embedded in the header of each quarterly file.

- Landing page: https://www.td.gov.hk/en/road_safety/road_traffic_accident_statistics/junction_blacksite_list/index.html
- The most explicit public rationale appears in **LegCo LCQ14, 2 July 2008** (then-Secretary Eva Cheng): TD "made reference to overseas practice"; "there are no common criteria or international standards" — i.e. it is an operational decision, not a statutory one.
  https://www.tlb.gov.hk/eng/legislative/transport/replies/land/2008/200807037.htm

→ **Implication for our enquiry:** asking "what is the original policy intention?" is FAIR GAME — it has never been publicly stated.

## 2. Official definition / threshold

Confirmed and apparently stable since at least 2008:

| Category | Threshold |
|---|---|
| **A(i)** | ≥ 6 accidents involving pedestrian injuries in past 12 months |
| **A(ii)** | ≥ 9 accidents involving any personal injury in past 12 months |
| **B** | ≥ 2 accidents involving fatalities in past 5 years |
| **C** *(NEW — added between Q4-2018 and Q3-2023, no announcement found)* | ≥ 8 accidents involving pedestrian injuries in past 3 years |

Source PDFs:
- Q4-2018 list (A+B only): https://www.td.gov.hk/filemanager/tc/content_2042/Q4-2018%20junctionblacksite_internet_chi.pdf
- Q3-2023 list (adds C): https://www.td.gov.hk/filemanager/tc/content_2042/Q3-2023%20junctionblacksite_internet_chi.pdf
- HKPF Offbeat #750: https://www.police.gov.hk/offbeat/750/eng/n08.htm

→ **The Category C addition is undocumented in any LegCo paper.** This is our single sharpest question.

## 3. What does TD actually DO at blackspot junctions?

**Most current source: LCQ13, 30 July 2025** (Secretary Mable Chan):

> "Between 2020 and 2024, the TD carried out road improvement works at approximately 70 traffic accident black spots... improvement works have been completed at over 50 of these sites. Among them, 30 locations recorded a reduction in overall traffic accidents and casualties by approximately 24 per cent and 30 per cent."

- URL: https://www.info.gov.hk/gia/general/202507/30/P2025073000192.htm
- Interventions listed (generic): signal adjustment, central dividers, pedestrian facilities, signage/markings.
- No junction-level breakdown. No timeline per site. No published MOU with HKPF on enforcement.

Audit Commission Report No. 46 Ch. 6 (March 2006) on road safety: covers accident investigation and law-enforcement metrics. **Does NOT review the blackspot classification or remediation workflow.** https://www.aud.gov.hk/pdf_e/e46ch06.pdf

HKPF "Blacksite Ownership Scheme" (Offbeat #750, ~2000s): only public description of a Police-side response protocol. Current status unclear.

Ombudsman: no direct investigation into TD's blackspot management found.

RSC annual reports: campaign/aggregate trends only, no junction-level interventions.
https://www.roadsafety.gov.hk/en/database/annual-reports

## 5. AADT / traffic volume at junction level

- **Annual Traffic Census** (road-section count stations, not junction approaches):
  https://atc.td.gov.hk/aadt  |  https://www.td.gov.hk/en/publications_and_press_releases/publications/free_publications/
- **Intelligent Road Network Package** GIS: https://www.td.gov.hk/en/public_services/intelligent_road_network_package/index.html
- Junction-approach turning-movement counts are **not** routinely published — must be requested under the Code.

## 6. Pre-2010 records: machine-readable?

**No.** 2004–2009 stats are HTML tables + PDF charts; data.gov.hk machine-readable releases begin ~2012.
- 2004 stats: https://www.td.gov.hk/en/road_safety/road_traffic_accident_statistics/2004/index.html
- 2022 dataspec: https://www.td.gov.hk/datagovhk_td/rt-accidentstat-2022/resources/accident_stat_2022_dataspec_eng.pdf

## 7. Pre-2014 vs post-2014 reporting break

No public explanation. C&SD's Feb 2025 feature only covers 2014-onwards without stating why. HKPF stats page splits at the same boundary. Likely a Traffic Information System change ~2013-14, never formally announced.
